How to Copy Discord ID

PC / Desktop

  1. Open Discord and go to User Settings (gear icon next to your username)
  2. Click on Account in the left sidebar
  3. Under "Account" section, find your Username and Discriminator
  4. Your Discord Tag format is: Username#0000
  5. Example: If your username is "ProGamer" and discriminator is "1234", enter ProGamer#1234

Mobile / iOS

  1. Open the Discord app and tap on the hamburger menu (three lines) in the top-left
  2. Tap on the gear icon (Settings) at the bottom right
  3. Tap on Account
  4. View your username and discriminator number
  5. Format: Username#0000 (e.g., ProGamer#1234)

Important Note

If you're using a new Discord account (after username migration), you won't have a discriminator. In that case, just enter your complete username as shown in your account settings. Discord changed to the new system where everyone has a unique username (e.g., pro gamer instead of ProGamer#1234).
