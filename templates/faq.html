FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I get my rank after payment?

Once payment is confirmed, join the server and your rank will be applied automatically within 5 minutes. Make sure to use the same Discord/Minecraft username during purchase.

Can I upgrade my rank later?

Yes! You only pay the difference when upgrading. Contact an admin on Discord for upgrades.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely! We use Razorpay, India's most trusted payment gateway. Your card details are never stored on our servers.

What if I change my mind?

We offer a 24-hour refund policy. Contact our Discord support team for assistance.

How long does monthly rank last?

Monthly ranks last for 30 days from the date of purchase. You'll need to renew to keep your rank and perks.

What happens to my rank if the server closes?

Lifetime ranks are valid for the duration the server is online. We cannot guarantee perpetual service, but we'll always try to keep the server running as long as possible.

Can I get a refund if I'm banned?

No. Violating server rules and receiving a ban does not qualify for a refund. All purchases are final as per our Terms & Conditions.

How do I contact support?

Join our Discord server and open a support ticket. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.
